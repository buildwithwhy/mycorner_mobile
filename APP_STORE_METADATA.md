### Short Version (for Google Play short description, 80 chars):

### Full Description:

Moving to London? MyCorner helps you explore and compare neighborhoods to find the perfect area for your lifestyle.

**Key Features:**

• **Explore Neighborhoods** - Browse detailed profiles of London neighborhoods with ratings for safety, transit, affordability, nightlife, and more.

• **Smart Comparisons** - Compare up to 3 neighborhoods side-by-side to see how they stack up across all metrics.

• **Personal Tracking** - Save favorites, mark places you've visited, create a shortlist, and add your own notes and ratings.

• **Commute Planning** - Add your work, school, or regular destinations to see how neighborhoods fit your daily routine.

• **Interactive Map** - View all neighborhoods on a map to understand their locations and proximity to your important places.

Whether you're a student, professional, or family looking for a new home in London, MyCorner gives you the insights you need to make the right choice.
